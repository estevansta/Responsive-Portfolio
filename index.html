About Me

Hi! My name is Estevan, I am a 23-year-old Photographer from Chicago that's making my way into the field of tech.

My background started in medicine where I graduated with a B.S. in Exercise Science and Health Promotion. I initially wanted to be a Physical Therapist but I decided it wasn't my path. Since then I've spent most of my time post-college being a Freelance Photographer, which allowed me to work with brands like Nike, Jordan Brand, Puma and Finishline as well as a handful of inspiring Influencers and Artists. It is my dream to work with people and brands that aspire to invest in the youth and next generations to express their art and further their creative abilities. I plan to use my creative eye and experience with working alongside brands to further my understanding of how to apply what I learn from this CodingBootcamp into practice.
